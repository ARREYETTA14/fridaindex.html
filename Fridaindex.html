Personal Information

When she finished high school, she went to did her Bachelor's degree in Nursing in Buea, Fako Division, South West Region and after 4 years, she graduated as a Bachelor of Nursing Science. She has had other professional achievements during her stay in the University such as, working as an intern in Limbe Regional Hospital, Laquantine Douala and she presently works in Solidarity Hospital Buea. It is also important to mention that, she is one of the active members of KOCAN organization that specializes in trying to kick out cancer from Cameroon. She has been a lorate in the Orange Excellence award on 2022.
